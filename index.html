What I Do

Most people waste time on repetitive, manual tasks that could easily be handled by intelligent agents. I build AI-powered automations using LangChain, local LLMs (via Ollama), and smart tools that solve real-world problems — from job applications to lead gen to client outreach.

Featured Project

🔁 SDR Job Application Agent – A LangChain-powered local AI agent that:

  • Scrapes job boards (Remotive, We Work Remotely, etc.)
  • Filters jobs based on a tailored CV
  • Generates personalized cover letters using LangChain + local LLMs
  • Sends job + cover letter to Telegram for review
  • Applies via email or form (with Playwright)
  • Tracks applications in Google Sheets

It’s like having a full-time job hunter working 24/7 — all running locally without OpenAI or paid APIs.
